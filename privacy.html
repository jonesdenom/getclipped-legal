Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2, 2025

HypeClipper ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

  • OAuth tokens from platforms such as YouTube and TikTok (to enable uploading on your behalf).
  • Basic profile information provided by those platforms (such as user ID and channel/account name).
  • Uploaded video files you submit for publishing.

How We Use Your Information

  • To upload your clips to YouTube and TikTok under your authenticated account.
  • To track clip counts for your usage statistics.
  • To maintain and improve our service.

Data Security

We store OAuth tokens securely in our database. Tokens are never shared with third parties and are used only for uploads you authorize.

Your Rights

You may revoke access at any time by removing getClipped from your authorized apps on YouTube or TikTok.
